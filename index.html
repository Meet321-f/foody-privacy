Privacy Policy - Foody

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Foody ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Foody mobile application.

By using Foody, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Eligibility & Children's Privacy

Foody is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If we discover that a user is under 13, we will immediately delete their account and associated data. Parents should contact us at meet321.dev@gmail.com if they believe their child's data has been collected.

3. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name and Email address for account creation
  • Profile information and images
  • Cooking preferences and dietary requirements
  • Recipes and content you generate using AI

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, model, and OS version
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Crash logs and diagnostic reports
  • IP address for security and analytics

We Do NOT Collect

  • Location data (GPS or precise location)
  • Payment or credit card information
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Health records or biometric data

4. Permissions We Request

To provide all features, our app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos for profile pictures or recipe images.
  • Photo Library: To select and upload existing photos from your device.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback and user experience improvements.

You can manage these permissions at any time via your device settings.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, manage, and secure your user account.
  • To generate personalized AI-powered recipes and images.
  • To improve App functionality, performance, and user interface.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To ensure App safety and prevent fraudulent activity.

6. Third-Party Services (AI & Infrastructure)

Foody uses trusted third-party services to provide core functionality:

  • Clerk: For secure user authentication. (Privacy Policy)
  • OpenRouter: For AI recipe generation. (Privacy Policy)
  • AIGurulab: For intelligent image generation.
  • TheMealDB: Public community database for recipe data.

Your personal identifying information (like your email) is never shared with AI text/image providers.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

Data Type Retention Period
Active Account Data While account is active
Deleted Account Data Deleted within 30 days of request
Crash Logs Stored for 90 days

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the App settings. All personal data will be purged within 30 days of deletion.

8. Data Security

We use HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit and follow industry-standard secure storage methods to protect your information. However, please be aware that no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Data Safety Declaration (Google Play)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:

  • Data Encrypted: Yes, data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.
  • Data Sharing: Data is shared only with functional service providers (Identity, AI), not for advertising.
  • User Control: You can request data deletion directly within the App.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw your consent for data processing by deleting your account.
