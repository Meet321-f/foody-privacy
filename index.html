Privacy Policy

App: Foody

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Foody collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app, including account features, AI recipe generation, recipe sharing, favorites, comments, reports, and feedback tools.

1. Who We Are

Foody is a recipe and food discovery app that lets users browse meals, save favorites, generate AI recipes, share their own recipes, comment on recipes, report content, and send feedback.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in or update your profile.
  • Recipe prompts you type for AI recipe suggestions and AI recipe generation.
  • Recipes you create and share, including title, description, ingredients, steps, image, servings, and diet type.
  • Comments you post on recipes.
  • Recipe reports you submit, including the reason for the report.
  • Feedback you send, including rating, message, feedback type, and optional name.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic account identifiers from Firebase Authentication, such as your user ID.
  • App activity related to favorites, AI recipe history, and profile sync.
  • Technical and diagnostic information such as error logs and service failures.
  • Notification permission status when Foody asks to send AI recipe ready notifications.

Foody does not ask for unnecessary personal data

  • No precise GPS location collection.
  • No payment card or banking information collection inside the app.
  • No contact list or address book collection.
  • No health record or biometric data collection.

3. Permissions We Request

  • Photo library / media access: to let you choose profile photos or recipe images from your device.
  • Notifications: to send optional alerts when an AI recipe is ready.
  • Haptics / vibration: to provide in-app feedback on supported devices.

You can manage these permissions from your device settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, maintain, and secure your Foody account.
  • To sync your profile details across supported app features.
  • To generate AI recipe suggestions and full AI recipes from your prompts.
  • To save your favorites and AI recipe history.
  • To publish user-submitted recipes and support community features such as comments and reports.
  • To review feedback, improve reliability, and fix bugs.
  • To protect the app, investigate misuse, and moderate harmful or abusive content.

5. Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Firebase Authentication: used for sign-in and account identity.
  • Firebase Firestore: used for cloud data such as profiles, recipes, comments, reports, and feedback.
  • Local device storage: Foody stores some data on your device using SQLite and AsyncStorage for profile data, cached content, favorites, onboarding status, and AI history.

6. Third-Party Services

Foody relies on third-party services to operate core features.

  • Google Sign-In: used when you sign in with Google and share approved profile details such as your name, email, and profile photo.
  • Firebase: used for authentication, cloud database, and cloud functions.
  • OpenRouter via backend function: used to process AI recipe prompts and generate AI recipe results.
  • TheMealDB: used for public meal search and meal detail content.

These providers operate under their own privacy terms. Foody only uses them to deliver app features and does not sell your personal data.

7. AI Features

When you use Foody AI, the recipe prompt or ingredient request you enter is sent through Foody's backend service to generate recipe suggestions or a full recipe. AI output may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for allergies or dietary needs, so you should review recipes before using them.

8. Sharing and Community Features

If you choose to share a recipe publicly, other users may be able to view details such as the recipe content, your display name, and profile image associated with that recipe. Comments and reports may also be stored with your user ID and display name for moderation and community safety.

9. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as it is needed to provide the app and operate core features.

Data type How long it may be kept
Account and profile data While your account remains active, unless earlier removal is required.
Shared recipes, comments, reports, and feedback For as long as needed to provide the feature, maintain records, or review abuse and safety issues.
Local device cache, favorites, and AI history Until removed by the app, deleted by you, or cleared from your device.
Diagnostic logs Only as long as reasonably needed for troubleshooting and service protection.

10. Account Deletion and Your Choices

You can delete your account from the app's settings screen. Deleting your account removes your authentication access, but some related records may remain for limited operational, moderation, legal, or security reasons where allowed by law.

You can also control app permissions, stop using community features, or contact us if you want help regarding your data.

11. Data Security

Foody uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information in transit and at rest. However, no app or online service can guarantee absolute security.

12. Children's Privacy

Foody is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.
